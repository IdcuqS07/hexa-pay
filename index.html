How HexaPay Works

Secure computation without exposing financial data.

HexaPay turns private financial input into verified workflow outcomes through a three-step FHE rail.

01

Seal the input

Balances, payment values, and invoice totals are encrypted before entering the workflow.

02

Compute privately

Approvals, treasury actions, and settlement logic execute while values remain protected.

03

Verify the outcome

Authorized viewers inspect sealed outputs locally without exposing the raw financial payload publicly.
